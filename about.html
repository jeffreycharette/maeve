OUR MISSION

Design Maeven is dedicated to promoting brands through effective, credible design. We are commited to providing a high calibre of creativity through brand consulting and one-on-one communication. Our aim is to form partnerships with businesses who seek to better the community.

MAEVE ROGERS

Maeve is a branding professional who left the agency world with a desire to redefine the client-creative relationship. She received her Bachelor of Fine Arts from University of North Texas in 2007 and is currently working towards certification from the University of Texas’ Small Business Success Program. In her spare time, she loves trying new restaurants and exploring the beautiful hill country. Maeve is an avid knitter and serves as Membership Chair of Capital City Scribes, Austin’s oldest Calligraphy Guild.

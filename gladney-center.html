Gladney Center For Adoption

Gladney is one of the nation’s leaders in global adoption. In 2008, most people only knew about Gladney’s domestic (U.S.) adoption programs.

To bring more awareness to Gladney’s expertise in international adoption, we created an adoption kit showcasing toys from different parts of the world. Each program is represented by a culturally relevant toy. The kit is tailored to each parent’s wants and needs.

In addition to the adoption kit, we created business cards, letterhead, folders, and greeting cards for office use.

Illustrations by Vincent Nguyen.
